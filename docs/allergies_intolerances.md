## B. Severe Intolerances / Adverse Reactions (avoid)
- **Opioids (incl. tramadol):** “very bad reactions” — avoid.
- **Gabapentin:** severe personality changes — avoid.
- **SSRIs/SNRIs (e.g., escitalopram, fluoxetine, venlafaxine, duloxetine):** uncontrollable/strange behavior — avoid.
- **Bupropion, Lithium, Depakote, Lamotrigine, Benzodiazepines:** poorly tolerated — avoid.
- **Antihypertensives:** lisinopril, metoprolol, amlodipine — poorly tolerated.
- **Food/biochemical:** high-histamine foods; **excess folate** — reproducible flare triggers.

## C. Helpful/Well-tolerated (see medication_effects.md for detail)
- Testosterone; Anastrozole; **Clonidine** (PRN); Hydroxyzine; Famotidine; **DAO** enzymes; **Creatine**; methylated B complex; SAMe; Probiotics; **HMB**; CBD/THC; Quetiapine (very low, HS).
